<p><strong>- Configuring Apache Spark</strong></p>

<p><strong>- Write Spark applications for visualization, machine learning, streaming SQL</strong></p>

<p><strong>- Resilient Distributed datasets</strong></p>

<p><strong>- Application Submission and Spark Driver</strong></p>

<p><strong>- Lambda Architecture</strong></p>

<p><strong>- Spark Streaming and Dstream</strong></p>

<p>Learn all this and much more in this unique course with abundant practical tips and theoretical rigor to master Apache Spark.</p>
